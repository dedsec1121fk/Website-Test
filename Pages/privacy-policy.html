Privacy Policy

Short and clear: what we collect, what we don’t, and how to contact us.

This website is static HTML. By default, we do not collect personal data directly. Your hosting provider may log basic technical data (IP, user agent) for security.

2) Cookies

We only store local preferences in your browser (theme and language) using localStorage. These are not shared.

3) External links

Links to Stripe, GitHub, Instagram, etc. follow their privacy policies. We are not responsible for third-party data handling.

4) Contact

If you have privacy questions, contact us from the Contact page.
