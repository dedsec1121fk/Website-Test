FAQ

Fast answers, responsible guidance, and a troubleshooting mindset.

Common questions

Is this project for beginners?
Yes. The site is designed to guide you from fundamentals to more advanced topics step by step.
Do I need root access on Android?
In most learning cases, no. A clean Termux setup is enough for many workflows. Root is optional and should be used carefully.
Is everything free?
The core content is meant to be accessible. Some optional premium extras may exist to support development.
How can I contribute?
Feedback, documentation, translations, design improvements, and testing are all welcome. See the collaborations page.

Responsible use

Clear boundaries keep the community safe.

What’s allowed
  • Learning on your own devices
  • Lab environments and CTF-style practice
  • Authorized testing with written permission
What’s not supported
  • Unauthorized access
  • Data theft, spying, or harassment
  • Anything illegal or harmful
If you’re unsure

Choose the safest path: don’t run it, don’t share it, and ask for clarification in a legal/ethical context.

Troubleshooting mindset

Reproduce the issue

Try to trigger it reliably. Write down steps.

Reduce variables

Change one thing at a time. Keep notes.

Ask with context

Share device info, errors, and what you tried. You’ll get better help faster.
