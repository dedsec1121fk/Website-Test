Choose your path

Different people show up for different reasons. Pick a route — DedSec will make the next step obvious.

I want to learn OSINT

Understand what’s publicly visible, how to verify sources, and how to stay safe while researching.

I want to check my network

Map your own devices, spot risky settings, and learn why certain scans matter (and when they don’t).

I want better privacy

Small changes that add up: safer browsing, stronger accounts, and cleaner digital habits.

I want to automate

Turn repeating tasks into scripts, learn by doing, and build a toolkit that feels like yours.

What makes DedSec different

Personal, not corporate

This project started as my own need: a phone-first toolkit that’s readable, practical, and fun to learn.

Clarity over chaos

You’ll see what a tool does, why it matters, and the safe, legal way to use it.

Built for real devices

Runs inside Termux on Android. Lightweight, transparent, and designed to work without fancy hardware.

Community-driven

Feedback shapes everything. If something feels confusing, we fix it. If it feels useful, we expand it.

A promise before you dive in

DedSec is for learning and authorized security testing. If you don’t own it, or you don’t have permission, you don’t touch it. No excuses.

If you want a place to start: open the tools page, pick one goal, and do the smallest safe step today.

Sponsor FAQ

New to GitHub? Here’s how to create an account and sponsor the project.

  1. Create a GitHub account: open GitHub Sign Up, enter your email, create a password, choose a username, and verify your email.
  2. Log in to GitHub.
  3. Tap the Sponsor button above (or open the sponsorship page) and pick a monthly amount.
  4. Choose a payment method (card/PayPal if available), confirm, and you’re done.
