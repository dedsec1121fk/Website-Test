DedSec Project started as a personal setup I used on Android to practice, test my own networks, and learn faster. Over time it became a clean, repeatable toolkit I could share with others.

If you’re curious but overwhelmed, you’re exactly who this is for. You don’t need to know everything. You just need the next step.

Use responsibly

DedSec Project is for education, learning, and authorized security testing only. Do not use it on networks, accounts, or systems you don’t own or don’t have explicit permission to test.
